# General FAQ

## Account & Access

**Q: How do I reset my corporate email password?**
A: Visit the IT Self-Service Portal at https://itportal.company.com/reset. See the full Password Reset Policy for details.

**Q: I'm locked out of my account. What do I do?**
A: After 5 failed login attempts, your account is automatically locked. Contact the IT Help Desk at ext. 4500 for immediate unlock during business hours.

**Q: How do I set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)?**
A: Download the Microsoft Authenticator app on your phone, then go to https://aka.ms/mfasetup and follow the prompts. You will need your corporate email credentials.

## Equipment

**Q: Can I use my personal laptop for work?**
A: The company supports a limited BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) program. You must enroll your device through the IT Portal and install the Mobile Device Management (MDM) agent. Contact IT for eligibility.

**Q: My monitor isn't working. What should I do?**
A: Try a different cable first (HDMI/DisplayPort). If the issue persists, submit a hardware ticket at https://itportal.company.com/hardware.

## HR & Benefits

**Q: How do I check my remaining leave balance?**
A: Log into the HR Portal at https://hrportal.company.com and navigate to "My Leave Balance" under the Time & Attendance section.

**Q: How do I update my emergency contact information?**
A: Go to https://hrportal.company.com/profile and update the "Emergency Contacts" section. Changes take effect immediately.

**Q: When is payday?**
A: Salaries are paid on the 25th of each month (or the previous business day if the 25th falls on a weekend or holiday). Direct deposit is processed overnight on the 24th.

**Q: How do I enroll in benefits (health, dental, vision)?**
A: Benefits enrollment is available during the annual Open Enrollment period (November 1-30) or within 30 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth of child, etc.). Visit https://hrportal.company.com/benefits.

## Workplace

**Q: What is the guest Wi-Fi password?**
A: The guest Wi-Fi network is "Company-Guest". No password is required — guests authenticate through a captive portal using their email address.

**Q: How do I book a conference room?**
A: Use the Outlook calendar to search for available rooms, or use the Room Finder add-in. Rooms can be booked up to 2 weeks in advance.

**Q: What are the office hours?**
A: Standard office hours are 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Flexible working arrangements are available with manager approval.
