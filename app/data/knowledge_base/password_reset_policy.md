# IT Password Reset Policy

## Overview
All employees must follow this procedure when requesting a password reset for any corporate system.

## Procedure
1. **Self-Service Reset**: Visit the IT Self-Service Portal at https://itportal.company.com/reset.
2. **Verification**: You will be asked to verify your identity using your employee ID and registered phone number.
3. **Temporary Password**: A temporary password will be sent to your registered email within 5 minutes.
4. **First Login**: Use the temporary password to log in. You will be prompted to create a new password.
5. **Password Requirements**: New passwords must be at least 12 characters, include uppercase, lowercase, a number, and a special character.

## Escalation
- If the self-service portal is unavailable, contact the IT Help Desk at ext. 4500 or email helpdesk@company.com.
- Account lockouts after 5 failed attempts require IT admin intervention — submit a Priority 1 ticket.

## Turnaround Time
- Self-service: Instant (automated)
- Help desk assisted: Within 30 minutes during business hours (8 AM – 6 PM)
- After hours: Next business day unless flagged as Priority 1

## Important Notes
- Passwords expire every 90 days. You will receive reminder emails 14, 7, and 1 day before expiration.
- Do NOT share your password with anyone, including IT staff. IT will never ask for your password.
