# Expense Reimbursement Process

## Overview
Employees may submit expense claims for business-related costs. All claims must comply with this policy to be reimbursed.

## Eligible Expenses
- **Travel**: Flights, trains, taxis, rideshares, and mileage (at $0.67/mile).
- **Accommodation**: Hotel stays for business trips (up to $200/night domestic, $350/night international).
- **Meals**: Business meals up to $75/person/meal. Client-facing meals up to $150/person/meal.
- **Office Supplies**: Pre-approved supplies under $100 do not require manager approval.
- **Training & Conferences**: Registration fees, travel, and materials for pre-approved professional development.

## Non-Eligible Expenses
- Personal entertainment or recreation
- Alcohol (unless part of an approved client entertainment expense)
- First-class airfare (business class requires VP approval for flights over 6 hours)
- Personal phone bills (unless on an approved BYOD plan)

## Submission Process
1. **Collect receipts**: Original receipts are required for all expenses over $25.
2. **Submit via HR Portal**: Go to https://hrportal.company.com/expenses within 30 days of the expense.
3. **Attach documentation**: Upload receipts and provide business justification for each line item.
4. **Manager approval**: Your direct manager must approve claims within 5 business days.
5. **Finance review**: Finance team reviews approved claims within 3 business days.

## Reimbursement Timeline
- **Standard**: Reimbursement is deposited within 10 business days of finance approval.
- **Express**: For amounts over $500, request express processing for reimbursement within 5 business days.

## Policy Violations
- Submitting false or inflated claims will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
- Repeated late submissions (beyond 30 days) may result in denial of future claims.
