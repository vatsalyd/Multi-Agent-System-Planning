# New Employee Onboarding Checklist

## Overview
Welcome to the company! This checklist covers everything you need to complete during your first two weeks. Your manager and HR buddy will assist you through this process.

## Day 1: Orientation
- [ ] Attend the Welcome Session with HR (9:00 AM, Conference Room A or via Zoom).
- [ ] Receive your employee badge, laptop, and welcome kit from the IT desk.
- [ ] Set up your corporate email and Active Directory account with IT support.
- [ ] Complete mandatory compliance training modules (available on the Learning Portal).
- [ ] Review and sign the Employee Handbook acknowledgment form.

## Days 2-3: Systems & Access
- [ ] Set up VPN access (see VPN Setup Guide).
- [ ] Request access to team-specific tools (Jira, Confluence, Slack channels) via the IT Portal.
- [ ] Configure Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) on your corporate account.
- [ ] Set up direct deposit for payroll via the HR Portal.
- [ ] Complete your employee profile with emergency contacts and tax documentation.

## Week 1: Team Integration
- [ ] Meet with your direct manager for a 1:1 goals-setting session.
- [ ] Schedule 30-minute introductory calls with key team members and stakeholders.
- [ ] Review team documentation and project backlogs.
- [ ] Complete any role-specific certification or training modules.

## Week 2: Ramp-Up
- [ ] Receive your first project assignment or shadowing schedule.
- [ ] Attend your first team standup and sprint planning meetings.
- [ ] Complete the "30-Day Onboarding Feedback" survey sent via email.
- [ ] Schedule a check-in with your HR buddy.

## Important Deadlines
- Tax and payroll forms: Must be completed within 3 business days of start date.
- Compliance training: Must be completed within 5 business days of start date.
- Probationary period: 90 days from start date.
