# Employee Leave Policy

## Overview
This policy covers all types of employee leave including annual leave, sick leave, parental leave, and unpaid leave.

## Annual Leave (Paid Time Off)
- **Entitlement**: Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
- **Accrual**: Leave accrues at 1.67 days per month.
- **Carry-over**: A maximum of 5 unused days may be carried over to the next calendar year.
- **Request Process**: Submit leave requests through the HR Portal at https://hrportal.company.com at least 5 business days in advance.
- **Approval**: Requires manager approval. Requests during peak periods (December, Q4 close) may be subject to team coverage requirements.

## Sick Leave
- **Entitlement**: 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
- **Documentation**: A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
- **Notification**: Notify your manager before your shift start time. Follow up with an email to your manager and HR within 24 hours.

## Parental Leave
- **Primary caregiver**: 16 weeks paid leave.
- **Secondary caregiver**: 4 weeks paid leave.
- **Eligibility**: Available after 12 months of continuous employment.
- **Application**: Submit application to HR at least 8 weeks before expected start date.

## Unpaid Leave
- **Eligibility**: Available after exhausting paid leave entitlements.
- **Maximum duration**: 30 consecutive business days.
- **Approval**: Requires both manager and HR director approval.
- **Benefits**: Health insurance continues during unpaid leave of up to 30 days.

## Public Holidays
The company observes 10 public holidays per year as per the regional office calendar published annually by HR.
